Live AI Pushup Counter

How to Use

  • Position yourself in front of your webcam in a pushup starting position
  • Make sure your full body is visible in the frame
  • Perform pushups at a steady pace
  • The counter will automatically track your pushups in real-time
  • The position status will show whether you're in the 'up' or 'down' position
  • The confidence score indicates how certain the AI is about its prediction
